React is a JavaScript library for building user interfaces.
It was developed by Facebook and is now maintained by Meta.

You can learn more at https://react.dev or email support@react.dev.

The component-based architecture makes it easy to reuse code.
However, you shouldn't overuse state management in small apps.

2. Creating your first component
